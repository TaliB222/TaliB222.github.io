About Me

I am a Ph.D. holder with a strong foundation in Biochemistry and molecular biology from Tel Aviv University and I am a Data Scientist passionate about machine learning, AI systems and End-to-End data-driven solutions. My doctoral studies focused on cellular signaling pathways in the brain (pituitary gland), where I developed expertise in experimental design, data analysis, and scientific communication. Following my Ph.D, I expanded my expertise into the field of genomic sequencing analysis, further strengthening my data-driven research capabilities.

At Tel Aviv Medical Center, I lead digital health and clinical data projects, where I design and implement data workflows, analyze complex medical datasets, and develop full-stack applications for clinical research.

My last projects include building AI-powered solutions such as LLM-based Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for querying clinical trial protocols, as well as data platforms used by research teams. With experience spanning translational research, data analysis, and software development, I bring a unique combination of domain expertise and technical capabilities. I am passionate about leveraging AI and data science to improve clinical decision-making and drive innovation in healthcare. Keeping up with the latest advancements in AI and data science is a key part of my journey.
